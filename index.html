San Clemente Water Heater Repair

Is your water heater leaking? Does it lack the capacity to meet your family’s needs? Nick Allen Plumbing offers professional support and cost effective solutions. As an established, licensed professional, I never rely on recently trained technicians. Rather, I personally complete each service call to ensure the job is done right and you're completely satisfied.

I install quality, American-made water heaters. All replacement services include at no additional cost:

  • Shut off valve repair
  • Gas supply lines and flex valves
  • Earthquake straps
  • Removal and haul away of your old water heater
  • Gas drip legs - measures that help ensure your home remains safe from water heater leaks and costly damage

My passion for quality service shows at Nick Allen Plumbing. I go the extra mile to ensure you remain a customer for life. Contact me to learn more about convenient, cost effective water heater replacement solutions for your family and to schedule your service appointment.

The water heater installation includes:

  • 1
    Removal and haul away of old heater.
  • 2
    Fixing the shut off valves, Gas supply line valve and flex line.
  • 3
    Installing proper amount of earthquake straps.
  • 4
    Gas drip leg.
  • 5
    A pan under the water heater to prevent water damage to home.
